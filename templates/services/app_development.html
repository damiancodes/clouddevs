Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about mobile app development

The development timeline varies depending on the complexity of the app. A simple app can take 2-3 months, while a more complex app with advanced features may take 4-6 months or longer. We provide a detailed timeline during the project planning phase.

Native apps are developed specifically for one platform (iOS or Android) using platform-specific programming languages. They typically offer the best performance and user experience but require separate codebases. Cross-platform apps are developed using frameworks like React Native or Flutter, allowing for a single codebase to work on both platforms, which can be more cost-effective but may have some limitations for highly specialized features.

App development costs vary widely based on complexity, features, and platforms. Simple apps may start around $10,000-$30,000, while more complex apps with advanced features can range from $50,000 to $250,000+. We provide detailed estimates based on your specific requirements during the consultation phase.

Yes, we provide full support for app store submission and publishing. We help prepare all necessary materials, including app descriptions, screenshots, and promotional videos, and guide you through the submission process for both the Apple App Store and Google Play Store.

We offer ongoing maintenance and support services to ensure your app continues to run smoothly. This includes bug fixes, performance optimization, compatibility updates for new OS versions, and feature enhancements.
